1. Universal Visit Flow (every visit)

On arrival
Wash/sanitize hands first and between species/enclosures.
Count pets, confirm all present and safe.
Quick home scan: temperature, open windows, hazards, plants/chemicals, chewed cords.
Fresh water for all species.
During visit
Feed per species below and Owner Notes.
Litter/spot clean, outdoor potty as applicable.
Enrichment/play/social time per species.
Medication per Owner Notes (verify dose/time, log immediately).
Before leaving
Final headcount; secure doors/gates/tanks/cages.
Lights/heaters/filters back on; timers correct.
Write the Daily Report (see template).

5. Emergency Protocol

Assess: Is the pet in immediate danger? Secure others.
1. Contact: Call owner → if no answer in 2–3 minutes and it’s urgent, call emergency vet.
2. Stabilize: Keep warm/cool as appropriate, limit movement, stop bleeding with clean pressure, avoid feeding.
3. Transport: Use carrier/leash; bring meds list.
4. Document: Time line, observations, photos, what you did.
